## Co-op Conversion Pattern
1. `Coop.EnableSyncingThisScript()` after `script_name`
2. `Coop.CollectNetworkPlayersForTheMission()` to get `$NETWORK_PLAYER[0..2]`
3. `Coop.TeleportPlayersToHostSafely(x, y, z)` after cutscenes / teleports
4. Give weapons/items to all network players via for loop
5. Mirror `Text.PrintNow` with `Coop.PrintNowForNetworkPlayer` in loops
6. Mirror blips/checkpoints with per-player Coop.Update*ForNetworkPlayer calls
7. Cleanup via `Coop.ClearAllEntityBlipsForNetworkPlayer`
